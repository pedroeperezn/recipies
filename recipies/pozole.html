Descritpion

"Pozole" is one of the most typical and traditional dishes of all mexican cuisine. It is a slightly spicy, rich, and thick broth that contains maize grains, pork or chicken meat, and is topped with lettuce and radish. This combination of flavours results in an exquisite culinary experience that everyone should try if they're passing through Mexico.

Preparing pozole might hear daunting at first, but it is actually a simple recipy. The complicated aspect of it is finding authentic and fresh ingredients. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of preparing an authentic red pozole from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 5 litters of water
  • 2 onions
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp of salt
  • 1 1/2 kg of pozole maize
  • 1/2 kg of pork rib
  • 1 kg of boneless pork leg
  • 5 laurel leafs
  • 14 guajillo peppers (remove seeds and soak in water for 20 min.)
  • 2 ancho peppers (remove seeds and soak in water for 20 min.)
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of black pepper (grounded)
  • Dry oregano
  • Powdered chile de arbol
  • Finely chopped lettuce
  • Sliced radish
  • Limes
  • Corn tostadas
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook the maize with the meat: First, boil 5 litters of water with one full onion, 4 garlic cloves, and salt. Once the water is boiling, add all your pozole maize and cook for 1.5 hours. Then, add all the meat and your laurel leafs. Let it boil for another 1.5 hours. Finally, remove the garlic cloves, laurel leafs, and set apart the pork meat for shredding and serving.
  2. Blend the peppers and seasoning to add it to the broth: Blend all peppers in 3 cups of water. Add 1/4 onion, 2 garlic cloves, the cumin, and the black pepper. Pass it through a strainer and add it to the broth. Let it cook for another 30 minutes.
  3. Serve: Once the pozole is cooked, serve the broth with maize grains, add your shredded meat, and finally garnish with fresh lettuce and radish slices. You can add chopped onions, oregano, and lime juice to taste. Enjoy with a tostada with sour cream on the side!
