  • Pozole

    "Pozole" is a slightly spicy, rich, and thick broth that contains maize grains, pork or chicken meat, and is topped with lettuce and radish.

  • Pambazos

    Pambazos are a pan fried potato puree sandwich dipped in pepper sauce. They are normally garnished with fresh lettuce, grinded fresh cheese, and sour cream.

  • Mexican Red Rice

    Rice boiled in a tomato broth which gives the color and taste to the rice. It is very common to it this version of rice as a side dish or as an extra ingredient in tacos.
